Where Modern Craftsmanship Meets Timeless Elegance

The Story of Aura

Founded in 2010 by master craftsman Arjun Verma, Aura Jewels began with a simple vision: to create jewellery that tells a personal story. We specialize in ethically sourced diamonds and pure 22k gold, blending traditional techniques with contemporary design to create pieces that are truly one-of-a-kind.

Our Speciality

Our passion lies in bespoke engagement rings. We work closely with our clients to bring their vision to life, ensuring every detail, from the cut of the diamond to the finish of the band, is absolute perfection. We also offer a curated collection of modern polki and temple jewellery.
