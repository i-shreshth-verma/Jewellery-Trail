Rajputana Gems

Carrying the Legacy of Royal Indian Craftsmanship

The Story of Rajputana

For over three generations, Rajputana Gems has been a custodian of royal Indian jewellery-making techniques. Our journey began in the bylanes of Jaipur, where our forefathers served as artisans to royal families. We bring that same dedication, detail, and heritage to our showroom in Greater Noida.

Our Speciality

We are renowned for our authentic Bridal and Trousseau collections. Our master karigars specialize in Kundan, Polki, and Meenakari work, creating timeless pieces that are not just ornaments, but heirlooms. Every piece is handcrafted, preserving a legacy of art that is centuries old.
