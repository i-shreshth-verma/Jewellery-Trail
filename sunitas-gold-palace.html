The Story of Sunita's

Sunita's Gold Palace is more than just a store; it's a part of the community. For over 30 years, our family has served the families of Greater Noida, building relationships based on honesty and trust. We believe in fair pricing and pure quality, ensuring that every customer leaves with a smile.

Our Speciality

We are experts in 22-karat hallmarked gold. Our focus is on providing beautiful, wearable jewellery for every important life event, from festivals like Diwali and Akshay Tritiya to everyday elegance. We offer a wide selection of chains, bangles, rings, and earrings, all with a guarantee of purity.
